Flood Response

Flood Response Group is a multidisciplinary team of researchers with expertise in flood modeling and forecasting, hazards and resilience, infrastructure resiliency, and computer engineering and sciences. We provide an integrative real-time flood hazard monitoring, modeling, and communication that improve public safety and the resilience and sustainability of critical infrastructure services, and community.

Our Mission

The mission of Flood Response consortium is to provide a scientific basis for the protection and connectivity of the communities with stakeholders by conducting integrative research, transferring technologies, and capacity building

Prepare

Enhancing the capability of communities to prepare for flooding

Respond

Withstanding and responding to flooding events.

Mitigate

Mitigating flood effects and assisting in relief and recovery
